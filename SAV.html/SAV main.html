HOW DOES SAV WORK?

SAV was created to gather information on relevant topics and people willing to take action around them. You read about what interests you, you decide the kind of project you want to start, and you call for a team. We provide you with the platform to develop your project and engage in active discussion with other teams that might be tackling related issues so that you can choose to merge and unite forces or else set specific limits on both projects in order to avoid overlap. Basically: we promote ideas and make sure your work is efficient, then we share your story to let it inspire the next great idea! What will you change?
